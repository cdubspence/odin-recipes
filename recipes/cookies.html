Cookie Recipe

Tessa loves to try and fatten me up. After any evening meal I am usually found eating some warm cookies made by my loving little wife.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Baking Powder
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Add all powders to a bowl and mix
  2. Add one egg and mix. Repeat
  3. Add Chocolate Chips
  4. Add milk and mix
  5. Ball up dough
  6. Preheat oven to 400
  7. Bake for 10-12 mins
  8. Enjoy!
